Akwa Ibom Chamber of Commerce - Website Plan

Site Name

Akwa Ibom Chamber of Commerce

This site name was selected to clearly represent the chamber of commerce in Akwa Ibom state, Nigeria, for branding, visibility, and recognition.

Site Purpose

The website provides a hub for local businesses to join the chamber, access membership information, connect through networking events, and stay updated on business news and opportunities in Akwa Ibom.

Scenarios

  • How can I apply for chamber membership and what are the benefits?
  • Where can I find information about upcoming business events and networking opportunities?

Color Scheme

  • Dark Green (#0a4d2e) - used for headers, buttons, and footer background
  • Gold (#ffd700) - used for accents, highlights, and call-to-action buttons

Typography

  • Roboto, sans-serif - used for body text and paragraphs
  • Roboto Bold - used for headings (h1, h2, h3) and buttons
